TUTORIAL


When the game begins, you are given a CATEGORY.
This category can be anything, from colors to U.S. Presidents.
In order to proceed to the next round (or complete the game),
you need to NAME FIVE of them.
When you input text correctly, it will become green and
you will proceed to the next box.
When you input text incorrectly, it will momentarily
flash red, signaling for you to try again.
Every letter you type subtracts one from your register.
You begin with three of every letter, and on every correct
answer, you get back random letters. When a round ends,
you will refill every letter under 2 to 2.
